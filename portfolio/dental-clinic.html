Dental Clinic

Dental Clinic is designed in modern style with a use of indirect lighting from alabaster. The space is planned in order to achieve privacy from the mezzanine floor. The use of PVC panels, concealed lights and glass panels with some artificial grass and other indoor plants gives space a feeling of being in a commercial space yet close to nature.
