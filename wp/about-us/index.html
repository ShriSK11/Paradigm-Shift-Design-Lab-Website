About Us

Our Vision

To make Paradigm Shift in Architecture & Interior Design

Paradigm Shift Design Lab was founded in the year 2019 by two architects Jayraj & Shreya with a passion of delivering the best to the industry.

 

Both the founders share the same interests in research, design & innovation. They believe in designing & creating interesting ideas & executing them in the affordable budgets for their clients. They have successfully achieved & delivered projects based on this principle.

 

Paradigm Shift Design Lab is the one stop design solution from building design, interior design, landscape design, product design and 3D renders. The firm has won several recognitions for the creativity in designing in competitions & projects. They thrive in the midst of complex projects that require depth of programming and cross-discipline collaboration. The team designs every project with an aim to resonate memory and a sense of discovery.

 

Paradigm Shift Design Lab continue to be different & distinguished by a clarity of form, elegance of execution and innovative use of materials.

Our Experts

Ar. Jayraj Ghatge

Principal Architect

B.Arch (2015), M. Arch in Computer Application (Digital Architecture) (2020)

“Parametricism”, the current trend connects all fields in architecture. Jayraj having done masters in Digital Architecture, aims to create a balance between research and practice. He admires math’s in design loves experimenting with forms based on mathematical equations.  He also believes the design can be fluidic even with the minimalistic approach. “So when there are 360 degrees, so why stick to 1”?

Ar. Shreya Ghatge

Principal Architect

B.Arch (2015), Mumbai University

Ar. Shreya specializes in designs developed using computational tools & techniques inspired by ‘Avant Garde’ style of Architecture. Shreya has worked in private firms as a junior Architect and has proved her ability to lead the projects individually. She has command in site (Interior Design) execution and management. Shreya has been practicing computational design methods and has developed furniture designs, architectural building façade designs during this time.
